Detailed Itinerary

  1. Morning:
    • Start your day at Lalbagh, a beautiful botanical garden.
    • Visit the Cubbon Park, a green oasis in the city center.
    • Explore the M.G. Road, a vibrant shopping and dining district.
  2. Afternoon:
    • Visit the Vidhana Soudha, the majestic state legislature building.
    • Take a walk through the Bangalore Palace, a historic royal residence.
  3. Evening:
    • Enjoy a traditional dinner at one of the city's renowned restaurants.
    • Stroll along the Brigade Road, a lively street with shops and cafes.
